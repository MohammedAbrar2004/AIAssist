MODULE CONTEXT
A Non-Compliance Report (NCR) documents a specific instance where a process, product, behaviour, or condition was found to violate an internal or external requirement. It is more targeted than a general incident — it identifies precisely what rule or standard was not met, where, and under what circumstances. NCRs typically trigger CAPAs and feed into compliance reporting.

FIELD REFERENCE

Witnesses: Individuals present at the time of the finding.

Severity: The seriousness of the violation.

Shift: The work shift during which the non-compliance occurred.
