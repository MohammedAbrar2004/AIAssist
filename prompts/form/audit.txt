MODULE CONTEXT
An audit is a formal, systematic review of whether the organisation's EHS practices, processes, or systems are functioning as intended and meeting required standards. Audits are broader than inspections — they assess whether the overall system is working, not just individual items. They may be internal or external, may cover a specific department or the whole organisation, and typically produce categorised findings.

Severity: The level of risk or significance associated with the audit scope.
Dates: The scheduled start and end dates of the audit.
Finding Types:
