MODULE CONTEXT
A near miss is an event that could have caused harm but did not — due to chance or because a control worked. Near misses are critical leading indicators of future incidents. Reporting them proactively is a sign of a healthy safety culture. The near miss module supports anonymous reporting to encourage open disclosure without fear of blame.
