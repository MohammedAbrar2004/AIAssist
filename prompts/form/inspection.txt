MODULE CONTEXT
Inspections are structured, planned checks carried out to verify that a workplace, process, or piece of equipment meets safety and operational standards. They are proactive activities — regular, scheduled, and methodical. The inspection module uses templates to define what will be checked, organised into sections and specific checklist items.

FIELD REFERENCE
Name: The title of the inspection.
Description: What the inspection is designed to check.
Task Type: The category of inspection activity.
Specific Task: The precise activity or check being performed.
Severity: The level of risk or compliance concern the inspection is designed to address.
Site: The facility the inspection applies to.
Effective Period: The date range during which this inspection template is active.
Sections: Groupings of related checklist items — e.g., Fire Safety Equipment, PPE Compliance.
Checklist Items: Individual checks within a section. Items may be mandatory, may require photographic evidence, and may trigger a finding if failed.
