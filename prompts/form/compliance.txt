MODULE CONTEXT
The compliance module manages the organisation's obligations under regulations, policies, standards, and internal requirements. Records may describe a control (a safeguard or procedure in place to manage a compliance obligation), an obligation (a specific regulatory or policy requirement the organisation must meet), or a task (a concrete work item that operationalises an obligation).

FIELD REFERENCE
Control fields:
  Code: The unique identifier for the control.
  Name: The name of the control measure.
  Type: The category of control — administrative, engineering, procedural, etc.
  Severity: The level of risk the control is designed to address.
  Frequency: How often the control must be performed or verified.
  Owner: The person responsible for the control.
  Clause / Regulation: The specific regulatory clause or internal policy the control relates to.
  Mandatory: Whether the control is required or optional.
  Effective / Expiry Dates: The period during which the control is active.

Obligation fields:
  Priority: The urgency of the compliance obligation.
  Type: The nature of the obligation — regulatory, internal, or policy-based.
  Category: Classification of the obligation.
  Title: A concise name for the requirement.
  Description: What the obligation entails.
  Status: The current compliance state.
  Due Date: When the obligation must be met.
  Risk Rating: The assessed risk level if the obligation is not met.
  Reviewer: The person responsible for reviewing compliance.

Task fields:
  Title: The name of the compliance task.
  Description: What the task involves.
  Due Date: When the task must be completed.
  Status: The current state of the task.
  Severity: How serious the issue is if the task is not completed.
  Version: The iteration of the task record.
  Assigned To: The person responsible for completing the task.
